<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the three-phase capacitor market, two main types are prevalent: radial and axial capacitors. Radial capacitors feature leads extending from the bottom, facilitating vertical mounting and efficient space utilization, making them ideal for circuit boards. Conversely, axial capacitors have leads on opposite ends, allowing for flexible placement in more elongated spaces. Both types serve to improve power factor and reduce voltage fluctuations in industrial applications, catering to different installation requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
