<p><strong>The U-Tube Manometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential U-Tube Manometer</li><li>Inverted U-Tube Manometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The U-Tube manometer market consists of various types, including Differential U-Tube Manometers and Inverted U-Tube Manometers. Differential U-Tube Manometers measure pressure differences between two points, making them ideal for applications in HVAC and fluid dynamics. Inverted U-Tube Manometers, on the other hand, are designed to measure low pressures by utilizing a vertical column of fluid, typically water or mercury. Both types are essential tools in industrial settings, ensuring accurate pressure measurements for process control and safety.</p></p>
