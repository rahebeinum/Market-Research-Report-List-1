<p><p>Tray sealing machines are classified into three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and fully automatic. Manual machines require operators to position trays and seal them, making them ideal for smaller operations or low-volume production. Semi-automatic machines automate part of the process, enhancing efficiency while still requiring some manual input, suitable for medium-scale operations. Fully automatic machines offer complete automation, handling high volumes with minimal human intervention, making them perfect for large-scale manufacturing and ensuring consistent output and packaging quality.</p></p>
