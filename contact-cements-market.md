<p><p>The contact cements market is categorized into three main types: CR (Chloroprene Rubber), SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene), and others. CR is known for its excellent adhesion and weather resistance, making it ideal for construction and automotive applications. SBS offers enhanced elasticity and strength, making it suitable for flexible bonding in various industries. Other types include various formulations and composites that cater to specific needs, such as specialty adhesives used in footwear, woodworking, and packaging, each providing tailored performance benefits.</p></p>
