<p><p>Leak Detection and Repair (LDAR) encompasses various technologies to identify and mitigate gas emissions in industrial settings. Portable analyzers provide rapid field measurements, while optical gas imaging uses infrared cameras to visualize gas leaks. Laser absorption spectroscopy detects specific gas concentrations with high sensitivity. Ambient/mobile leak monitoring involves stationary or mobile units for continuous assessment, whereas acoustic leak detectors identify leaks based on sound emissions. Other market types may include chemical sniffers or software solutions for leak management.</p></p>
