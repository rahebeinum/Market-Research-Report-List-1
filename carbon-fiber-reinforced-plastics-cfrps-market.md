<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Plastics (CFRPs) are composite materials that combine carbon fibers with plastic matrices, offering high strength-to-weight ratios and enhanced mechanical properties. The CFRP market is divided into two main types: thermosetting CFRPs, which cure into a rigid form when heated, providing excellent structural integrity but requiring longer processing times; and thermoplastic CFRPs, which can be reshaped upon heating, allowing for quicker production and recyclability, making them suitable for various applications, including automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
