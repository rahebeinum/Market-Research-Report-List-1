<p><p>The positive displacement meter market comprises several types, including diaphragm and gear types, alongside other specialized variants. Diaphragm meters utilize flexible membranes to measure fluid flow accurately, commonly applied in residential and light commercial settings. Gear meters employ rotating gears to quantify flow, often used in industrial applications for their reliability with varying viscosities. Other types encompass piston, oval gear, and nutating disc meters, catering to diverse applications and offering distinct advantages based on fluid characteristics and measurement requirements.</p></p>
