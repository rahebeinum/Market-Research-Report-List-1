<p><p>The electronic pet fence and containment market primarily consists of two types: in-ground fences and wireless fences. In-ground fences involve a buried wire that creates a defined boundary, providing customizable containment based on the pet's behavior and yard layout. Wireless fences, on the other hand, utilize radio signals from a transmitter to establish a circular boundary, allowing pets to roam freely within a designated area. Both types aim to enhance pet safety and freedom while preventing escapades and potential hazards.</p></p>
