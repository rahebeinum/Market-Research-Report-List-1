<p><p>The Portable Near Infrared (NIR) Analyzers market is categorized primarily into FT-NIR, AOTF-NIR, and other types. AOTF-NIR (Acousto-Optic Tunable Filter Near Infrared) analyzers offer fast, on-the-fly measurements suitable for field applications, emphasizing portability. Other types may include simpler handheld devices that balance cost and functionality for routine quality control in various industries such as agriculture, pharmaceuticals, and food processing.</p></p>
