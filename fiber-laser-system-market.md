<p><p>The fiber-laser system market can be categorized into four main types. Crystal fiber lasers utilize crystals to generate high-power laser beams, known for their efficiency and beam quality. Nonlinear fiber lasers exploit nonlinear optical effects to enhance performance and generate diverse wavelengths. Rare earth-doped fiber lasers incorporate rare earth elements in the fiber to achieve high power and stability, commonly used for industrial applications. Lastly, plastic fiber lasers use polymer fibers, offering flexibility and lightweight design, making them suitable for specific applications in telecommunications and sensing.</p></p>
