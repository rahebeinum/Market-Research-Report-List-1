<p><p>Organic bread flour for home cooking is produced from organically grown grains and is primarily available in two market types: machine-milled flour and stone-ground flour. Machine-milled flour is processed using modern milling techniques, resulting in a fine, uniform texture suitable for various bread recipes. In contrast, stone-ground flour is produced by grinding grains between stones, preserving more nutrients and flavor. Both types cater to consumers seeking healthier, sustainably sourced ingredients for crafting homemade bread.</p></p>
