<p><p>The Stretch Blow Molding PET market is categorized into two primary processes: the Single-Stage and Two-Stage processes. The Single-Stage process combines preform injection and blow molding in one step, optimizing time and reducing costs, making it suitable for smaller production runs. Conversely, the Two-Stage process separates preform manufacturing from the blow molding stage, allowing for higher output and more complex designs. This method is ideal for larger production volumes, ensuring better quality and precision in the final products.</p></p>
