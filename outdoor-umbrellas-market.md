<p><p>The outdoor umbrellas market is categorized into three main types: center pole umbrellas, offset pole umbrellas, and wall-mounted umbrellas. Center pole umbrellas feature a central support, ideal for patios and gardens, providing shade directly above. Offset pole umbrellas have a side support, offering versatile positioning and greater coverage without obstructing tables or seating. Wall-mounted umbrellas attach directly to walls, maximizing space and providing shade in smaller areas like balconies or patios. Each type meets specific outdoor shading needs.</p></p>
