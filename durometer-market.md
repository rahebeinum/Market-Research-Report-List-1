<p><strong>The Durometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brinell Durometer</li><li>Vickers Durometer</li><li>Rockwell Durometer</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The durometer market is segmented into various types, including Brinell, Vickers, Rockwell, and others. Brinell durometers measure hardness through a spherical indenter, ideal for materials with coarse structure. Vickers durometers utilize a diamond indenter, suitable for all materials, providing precise measurements across a range of hardness. Rockwell durometers offer quick results using varying loads for different scales, making them popular in manufacturing. Other types may include Shore durometers, which measure softer materials. Each method serves distinct applications for assessing material hardness.</p></p>
