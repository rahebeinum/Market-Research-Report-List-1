<p><p>The bulk cocoa market primarily consists of three main products: cocoa liquor, cocoa butter, and cocoa powder. Cocoa liquor is a thick paste made from ground cocoa beans, serving as the base for chocolate production. Cocoa butter, the fat extracted from cocoa beans, is essential for smooth chocolate texture and is also used in cosmetics. Cocoa powder, derived from the solid leftover after extracting cocoa butter, is widely used in baking, beverages, and confections, offering rich flavor and color.</p></p>
