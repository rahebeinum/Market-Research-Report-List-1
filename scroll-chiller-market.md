It employs two interleaved spirals, where one is fixed while the other orbits around it, effectively compressing refrigerant gas and facilitating heat exchange. <p><p>The scroll chiller market is primarily divided into water-cooled and air-cooled chillers. Water-cooled scroll chillers use water from cooling towers to dissipate heat, making them efficient for large installations where water supply is abundant. In contrast, air-cooled scroll chillers utilize ambient air for heat dissipation, offering easier installation and lower maintenance, ideal for smaller spaces or areas with limited water access. Both types serve various applications in commercial and industrial sectors, driven by energy efficiency and cooling demands.</p></p>
