<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Rubber (NBR) is categorized based on acrylonitrile (AN) content, which influences its properties. High AN Content (36-45%) provides strong chemical resistance and durability for industrial applications. Medium-high AN Content (31-35%) balances flexibility and resistance, suitable for automotive seals. Medium-low AN Content (26-30%) offers moderate resistance with better low-temperature performance. Low AN Content (15-25%) ensures superior flexibility and cold resistance, used in low-temperature environments. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
